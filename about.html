Praharsha Publications was founded in 2021 by a group of literature enthusiasts with a combined experience of more than thirty years in publishing. Our mission is to bring out high quality content aligned to Sanatana-dharma presented in a readable and charming form. When we produce a book, we pay special attention to every detail and endeavour to make it as affordable as possible for the reader. Classical literature and classical arts are the primary focus areas of Praharsha.
