The choicest verses of Lilashuka's shrikrishnakarnamritam, one of the finest works in Sanskrit literature, are presented here with translation and exposition of the poetic beauty. The entire work has been structured in the form of an informal conversation set in the glorious era of the Vijayanagara Empire.
