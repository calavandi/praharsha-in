This curation by Srinidhi Ramachandra and Hari Ravikumar includes the original Sanskrit text of Bhasa's Svapna-vasavadatta (based on the manuscripts discovered by T Ganapati Shastri), a remarkable English translation by V S Sukthankar, an overview of the play by M Hiriyanna, detailed additional notes, and ten appendices. This is the perfect companion to any student of Sanskrit who wishes to study this drama.
