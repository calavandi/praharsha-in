This khanda-kavya transforms the historic 1990 Rama Ratha Yatra into a classical Sanskrit poetic journey. Drawing inspiration from Kalidasa's Raghuvamsha and Meghaduta, it traverses India's landscapes — its mountains, rivers, cities, and people — through the eyes of a moving chariot. Blending historical realism with poetic imagination, the work re-affirms Sanskrit's vitality as a living medium capable of illuminating contemporary history. Includes an English translation and end-notes.
